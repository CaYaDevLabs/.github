## About

**CaYaDev Labs** is the experimental research and engineering division of
[CaYaDev](https://cayadev.com).

We develop open-source software, artificial intelligence systems, developer
tools, hardware projects and early-stage technologies. Projects published here
may be experimental, incomplete or subject to significant changes.

## Areas of Work

- Artificial intelligence and AI systems
- Developer tools and automation
- Creative software and interactive technologies
- Hardware and electronics
- Experimental research and prototypes

## Project Status

Repositories may use the following development stages:

- `Concept` — Early idea or research
- `Prototype` — Initial working implementation
- `Experimental` — Actively tested and developed
- `Beta` — Public testing stage
- `Completed` — Finished experiment or research
- `Archived` — Discontinued or preserved project

## Main Organization

Stable, official and actively maintained CaYaDev projects are published under
[CaYaDevHQ](https://github.com/CaYaDevHQ).
